Our Story

We had a pretty non-traditional having met online. At first we were quite cautious as we were both very new to all of this, but very quickly become comfortable with eachother. Soon we were on the phone all the time, anytime we had a spare moment we would see if the other was free for a chat. That first summer was the first time we met, and even though it was only a few days we knew there was something special. Since then, even though we were long distance we both made trips to see eachother and started falling in love. Fast forward to 2019, and we decided we didnt want to spend anymore time apart and on September 9th...She said yes!
